Online payments are processed securely through our trusted payment and checkout partner (Shiprocket Checkout / Fastrr and its associated payment gateways). Your payment card or banking details are handled directly by the payment processor and are not stored on our servers.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with service providers essential to running our business (payment processors, logistics partners, and analytics providers) or when required by law.
