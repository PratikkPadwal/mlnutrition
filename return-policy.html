Return & Exchange Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

We want you to shop with confidence. As we sell health supplements and consumable products, returns are accepted only under specific conditions described below, in line with hygiene and safety standards.

1. Return Eligibility

You may request a return or replacement within 48 hours of delivery if:

  • You received a damaged or tampered product.
  • You received an expired or near-expiry product.
  • You received the wrong product or wrong flavour/variant.
  • An item was missing from your order.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For health, hygiene, and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of:

  • Products that have been opened, used, or had their seal broken (unless they were defective or damaged on arrival).
  • Products returned without original packaging, labels, and proof of purchase.
  • Items reported after the 48-hour window.
  • Products damaged due to misuse or improper storage by the customer.

3. How to Raise a Return

  1. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 83293 84588 within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Share your order number and clear photos/video of the product and packaging (including an unboxing video where possible).
  3. Our team will verify your request and arrange a pickup or guide you on returning the item.

4. Replacement or Refund

Once the returned product is received and inspected, we will offer a replacement of the same item or a refund as per our Refund & Cancellation Policy. If a replacement is unavailable, a full refund will be issued.

5. Exchange

Exchanges (for a different flavour or variant) are subject to stock availability and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out to us before initiating any exchange.
