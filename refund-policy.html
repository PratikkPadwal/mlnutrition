Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This policy explains when and how order cancellations and refunds are handled at ML Nutrition. Please read it alongside our Return & Exchange Policy.

1. Order Cancellation

  • You may request a cancellation before your order is dispatched by contacting us on WhatsApp with your order details.
  • Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled, but it may be eligible for return as per our Return & Exchange Policy.
  • We reserve the right to cancel any order due to stock unavailability, pricing errors, or suspected fraud. In such cases, a full refund will be issued.

2. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Order cancelled before dispatch.
  • Product received damaged, defective, expired, or incorrect (subject to verification).
  • Order undeliverable or lost in transit.

Note: For hygiene and safety reasons, opened or used health supplements are not eligible for refund unless they are damaged, defective, or wrongly delivered.

3. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method for prepaid orders.
  • For COD orders, refunds are processed via bank transfer/UPI to the account details you provide.
  • Refunds are typically completed within 5–7 business days after approval, though the time for the amount to reflect may vary by bank.

4. Non-Refundable Charges

Shipping charges and COD handling fees (if any) are non-refundable, except where the return is due to our error (damaged, defective, or wrong product).

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a cancellation or refund, contact us on WhatsApp at +91 83293 84588 with your order number and reason. Our team will guide you through the next steps.
