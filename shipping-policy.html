Shipping Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

At ML Nutrition, we aim to deliver your supplements quickly, safely, and reliably across India. This policy explains our shipping timelines, charges, and processes.

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Metro & major cities: 2–5 business days.
  • Other locations: 5–8 business days.
  • Remote/rural areas: may take additional time depending on courier serviceability.

Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, weather, or other circumstances beyond our control.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges (if any) are calculated at checkout based on your delivery location, order weight, and the selected payment method (prepaid or Cash on Delivery). Any applicable charges will be clearly displayed before you complete your order.

4. Shipping Partner

We ship our orders through Shiprocket and its network of trusted courier partners (such as Delhivery, Bluedart, Xpressbees, and others). The courier is assigned automatically based on serviceability and speed for your pincode.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link and AWB number via SMS, WhatsApp, or email. You can use this to track your shipment in real time.

6. Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD may be available for eligible pincodes and order values. A nominal COD handling fee may apply and will be shown at checkout.

7. Delivery Issues

  • Please ensure your shipping address and phone number are accurate and complete. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery due to incorrect details.
  • If delivery fails after multiple attempts or the package is returned to us (RTO), we will contact you to arrange re-shipment or a refund as applicable.

8. Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or with missing items, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos/video of the package. See our Return & Exchange Policy for details.
